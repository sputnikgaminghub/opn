Start with your wallet Safety notes

End-to-end flow

  1. Open the app and choose Airdrop or Presale.
  2. Airdrop: enter your Ethereum wallet → check eligibility → (optional) claim.
  3. Dashboard: view referral code, stats, achievements, and withdrawals progress.
  4. Presale: use the calculator → pick a payment method → follow the instructions shown in-app.

Airdrop totals

Your airdrop total is built from multiple components:

  • Base amount: 1005 OPN
  • Referrals: 121 OPN per successful referral
  • Achievement bonuses (milestones)
  • Approved task points
  • Claim-window earnings (optional)

Tasks (proof-based)

The app supports a task system where you submit a proof URL. Submissions are reviewed and can be approved or declined:

  • One submission per task (resubmission allowed only if declined).
  • Approved tasks move into a completed archive.
  • Task points accumulate and are displayed on your dashboard.

Withdrawals

Withdrawals are gated to protect the system:

  • Referral requirement: 7 referrals
  • Open date (shown in-app)
  • Optional network fee validation (ETH / BNB)
