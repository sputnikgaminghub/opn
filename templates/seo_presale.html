Presale tiers (bonus structure)

  • Tier 1: 0.1–1 ETH → +20% bonus tokens
  • Tier 2: 1–5 ETH → +25% bonus tokens
  • Tier 3: 5+ ETH → +30% bonus tokens

The calculator in the app estimates tokens received and bonus amounts automatically.

Token conversion (calculator)

The app uses an internal conversion rate of:

1 ETH = 100,000 OPN

The calculator then applies the tier bonus based on your entered amount.

Payment methods

  • Manual: send ETH from your wallet to the presale address shown in-app.
  • Web3 gateway: pay with ETH, BNB, or USDT (ERC20 / BEP20).
  • Solana: optional Solana presale path (shown in-app if configured).

Minimums

  • Web3 gateway: $50 minimum (USD input).
  • NOWPayments donation option: minimum noted in-app (currently $50).

Verification & records

The platform stores presale transactions and uses network-aware verification logic (e.g., Ethereum vs BSC) when available.

  • Transactions can be recorded with wallet address, amount (USD + crypto), network, and TX hash.
  • The automated presale ledger summarizes totals and recent transactions.

Always verify you are using the official address shown inside the app before sending funds.
