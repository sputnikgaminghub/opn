How referrals are counted

  • You receive +121 OPN for each successful referral.
  • A referral is “successful” only when the referred user claims using your referral code.
  • Link clicks don’t count by themselves — claims do.

Your referral code

When you enter your wallet in the airdrop section, the system generates (or retrieves) a unique referral code for your wallet.

Share the code or your referral link from the dashboard.

Active referrals

The app marks a referrer as “active” once they reach 2+ referrals. This is used for network stats and ranking views.

Withdrawals requirement

Withdrawals are locked until you reach 7 referrals. You can track your progress in the Withdrawals tab inside the airdrop dashboard.

See the withdrawals rules →

Milestones & achievement bonuses

Referral milestones can unlock extra rewards automatically:

  • 1 referral: First Referral — 11 OPN
  • 3 referrals: Network Builder — 111 OPN
  • 5 referrals: Referral Master — 500 OPN
  • 6 referrals: Ready to Cash Out — 1500 OPN
