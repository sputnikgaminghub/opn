What the site will never ask you for

  • Your seed phrase, private key, or recovery words.
  • Remote access to your device or “support” screen-sharing to claim tokens.
  • Signing unexpected approvals you didn’t initiate yourself.

How the platform reduces abuse

  • Rate limits on wallet checks and claims (to reduce spam/bots).
  • IP-based limits (max wallets per IP) with temporary restrictions on abuse.
  • One-claim-per-wallet enforcement; repeat checks show “already claimed” and a breakdown.

Withdrawal safety

  • Withdrawals are gated by referral requirements and an “open at” date.
  • Network fees (when enabled) are small and chain-specific; you should see them clearly in-app before submitting.
  • Always verify URLs and contract/wallet addresses from the official app UI.

Phishing checklist

  • Type the domain yourself (avoid DMs with “claim now” links).
  • Verify the address shown in-app before sending funds.
  • If anything feels off, stop and contact support via the official contact page.
